About Thirumathi Kart

Where Trichy’s women meet modern commerce

Thirumathi Kart is a women-first digital marketplace crafted in collaboration with NIT Trichy to bring neighbourhood businesses online.

Thirumathi Kart is an end-to-end e-commerce platform dedicated to women entrepreneurs in and around Trichy. From home-based vendors and self-help groups to small neighbourhood stores, the platform makes it simple for women to list products, manage inventory and fulfil orders digitally.

Built by a team of NIT Trichy students, the project combines thoughtful user experience with a robust technology stack to create reliable income channels and greater visibility for women-led businesses.

Simple onboarding, vernacular touchpoints and assisted setup to help first-time digital entrepreneurs feel at home.
Hyperlocal delivery model that keeps logistics lean while ensuring fresh groceries, produce and home‑made foods.
Integrated buyer, seller and delivery apps that keep every stakeholder informed in real time.
Co-created with mentors and faculty at NIT Trichy to align technology innovation with social impact.

How Thirumathi Kart supports women

Beyond transactions, Thirumathi Kart is a digital bridge that connects women-led businesses in Trichy to families, offices and hostels across the city.

  • Gives visibility to local brands, self-help groups and home kitchens.
  • Reduces operational friction with easy order, payment and delivery flows.
  • Encourages financial independence and flexible work opportunities.
  • Acts as a living lab for NIT Trichy students to apply engineering for societal good.
